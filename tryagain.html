NDA IN VEX

Notre Dame Academy has participated in the VEX competition program since January of 2015, starting off with two teams, on the 18th of November they started their second team, Team 3890B.

A little bit about VEX

VEX Robotics Competition is the largest middle and high school robotics competition in the world. Standard matches are composed of two alliances of two teams each competing against each other. There is one robot and one driver per team playing for a limited amount of time. The games include an autonomous section in which the teams are autonomously drive their robots using programming skills. There are currently over 10,000 teams from 32 countries playing in 750 tournaments.
