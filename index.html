TEAM 8390

Hello! We're Team 8390, also known as R2G2, which stands for Robotics, Rockets, Girls and Gryphons. We're an all-girls Robotics and Rocketry team based in Los Angeles, California. We were found in January 2014 and consist of students from Notre Dame Academy, an all-girls school.
